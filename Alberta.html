The City of Calgary

Chinook Mall:


Chinook Mall is Calgary's largest mall located south of the city.
The mall has a large variety of stores as well as a Cineplex Movie theatre!

Calgary Stampede:

Also known as "The Greatest Outdoor show on Earth" The Calgary Stampede is arguably the best tourist attraction in the city.
This event comes around every summer and draws thousands of visitors.

The event provides Amusment Rides, food concessions, concerts etc.

The City of Edmonton

West Edmonton Mall:

The iconic West Edmonton Mall is the largest mall in North America and is the leading tourist attraction for the city.
This massive shopping mall includes a Water park, an indoor Amusment park, a large selection of shops, an indoor pirate ship, three food courts, a hotel and Mini Golf.

Fort Edmonton Park:

A theme park that recreates Edmonton locations from different eras.
The park includes a train, carnival games and a Ferris Wheel.

The Town of Banff/National Park

This National Park is located an hour west of Calgary. There are trails and a Hot Springs with a beautiful view of the Rocky Mountains.

Jasper National Park

This National Park is the largest park in the Rocky Mountains and includes Forestry, mountains and lakes. Visitors can also canoe and hike.
